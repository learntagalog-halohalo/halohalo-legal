Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

HaloHalo ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information through the App:

  • Learning progress (completed lessons, XP, streaks, earned badges)
  • Usage analytics (launch counts, screen views, in-app actions)
  • Crash and error reports (device model, OS version, error details)
  • Subscription purchase records (plan, purchase date)

We do not collect personally identifying information such as your email address, real name, or phone number.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

Your learning progress is stored locally on your device by default.

Usage analytics, crash reports, and purchase records are transmitted and stored in anonymized form through the third-party services listed below.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the App
  • To investigate and fix bugs
  • To manage subscriptions
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve quality

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services. Each service handles data under its own privacy policy.

  • Google Firebase (Analytics / Crashlytics): usage analytics and crash monitoring
  • RevenueCat: subscription management
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: payment processing
  • ElevenLabs: text-to-speech generation for learning audio
  • open.er-api.com: currency exchange rate data

5. Sharing With Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except when required by law.

6. Data Security

We apply reasonable security measures to protect the data we collect. However, no method of internet transmission can be guaranteed as fully secure.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all data stored on your device by uninstalling the App. To request deletion of anonymized analytics already sent to third-party services, please contact us.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out to learntagalog.halohalo@gmail.com.
